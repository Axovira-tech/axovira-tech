Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a project cost?

Every project is different. A simple landing page costs significantly less than a full SaaS platform. We provide a detailed estimate after our free discovery call so there are zero surprises.

How long does development take?

Typical projects range from 4–12 weeks depending on complexity. We work in agile sprints with weekly demos so you see progress from day one, not just at the end.

What technologies do you use?

We pick the best tool for each job: React/Next.js for web, Flutter/React Native for mobile, Node.js/Python for backends, and AWS/GCP for cloud infrastructure.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. After launch, we offer maintenance packages that include bug fixes, feature additions, performance monitoring, and scaling support. We don't disappear after deployment.

Can I see your past work?

Absolutely. During our discovery call, we'll share relevant case studies and live products we've built for clients in your industry.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — many of our clients are founders building their first product. We help you prioritize the right features for your MVP and scale from there.
